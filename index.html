The Process

How SymplClip Works

1
Upload Your Video
Drag and drop or browse for your video. MP4, MOV, WebM, AVI, and MKV supported up to 4GB. Your file never leaves your device — everything runs in your browser.
🔒 Processed locally · Never uploaded to our servers
2
Choose Your Detection Mode
Manual Split divides your video into equal segments. Highlight Detect finds the most energetic moments. AI Detect runs the full pipeline — transcription, moment detection, grading, and captions all in one pass.
⭐ AI Detect included on Founder Special and Pro
3
Review Your Clips
Your clips appear in a grid. AI Detect clips show a grade, a reason for selection, and a caption preview. Click any clip to open the editor.
4
Edit and Refine
Three-panel editor with trim controls, format selection, volume, speed, and captions. Cut Left, Cut Right, or use the scissors tool to remove middle sections. The timeline shows your edits in real time.
5
Download Your Clips
Download individual clips or add them to the queue and download everything at once. True MP4 output — plays everywhere, accepted by every platform.

What Is SymplClip

SymplClip is a browser-based video clip maker built for creators, marketers, podcasters, and anyone who needs to turn long video into short shareable content fast. No software to install. No account required to start. No subscription trap.

Upload your video, choose how to cut it, and download your clips. SymplClip is built and maintained by Super Sympl Tech.

Our Philosophy

Most video editing tools are built for editors. SymplClip is built for people who are not editors — people who have great content and need to get it out fast. The tool should do the heavy work so you can focus on what you do best.

Pay as you go. No subscriptions. No annual commitments. Use it when you need it.

Disclaimers
